The Best Movies According to Mayank

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spider Man Across The Spider-Verse

One of the Best Animated movies released in a long time with animation that is far ahead of its competitors.

Top Gun:Maverick

One of the best Action-Thriller movie released in the last decade.

Oppenheimer

“Oppenheimer” is a great achievement in formal and conceptual terms, and fully absorbing, but Nolan's filmmaking is, crucially, in service to the history that it relates.
